Crispy Honey Chilli Chicken

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
Chicken 500 g
Flour 200 g
Salt To taste
Pepper To taste
Soy sauce 2 tbsp
White wine vinegar 3 tsp
Honey 50 g
Garlic 3 cloves
Sriracha chilli garlic sauce 2-3 tbsp
Rice or noodles 250 g

Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken breast into thin strips
  2. Season the chcicken strips with salt and pepper and coat each piece with flour
  3. Pre-heat the oil and fry the coated chicken pieces until light brown
  4. Add minced garlic to the pan
  5. While chicken is frying, make the sauce: mix the soy sauce, white wine vinegar, honey and sriracha chilli garlic sauce together
  6. Pour the sauce mixture in and stir until the sauce starts to thicken a bit
  7. Serve with rice or noodles
